A Journey That Started With One Cab

RJ Travels began with a simple mission — to make travel easier, safer, and more dependable for every Indian. What started with one car and one driver in Ahmedabad has today become a trusted name in the travel industry across Gujarat.

We weren’t just building a taxi service—we were building a relationship of trust, one trip at a time. From helping families reach weddings in distant towns, to enabling solo travelers discover the unexplored, RJ Travels became more than a cab company. We became travel companions for thousands.

What Drives Us?

We believe every trip—short or long—is a story waiting to unfold. And every customer is a guest we’re privileged to serve. That’s why our drivers are more than chauffeurs—they're guides, caretakers, and problem-solvers on the road.

With 24x7 support, verified drivers, fixed pricing, and a growing network of routes, we bring reliability back to road travel—because we know you deserve better than last-minute cancellations or unpredictable fares.
